I build AI agents that work when a regulator is watching. Most don’t. I’ve spent the last decade closing the gap between AI demos and AI in production.

I ship production AI systems for governments and financial institutions — clients who cannot afford a wrong answer. I have built and led the organizations that sustain those systems at scale: 80-person AI teams, governance frameworks, evaluation pipelines. The technical problem is rarely the hard part. The hard part is context management — making sure every person and every system has the right information to make the right decision.
